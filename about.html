ABOUT ME

Builder. Researcher. Entrepreneur.

THE SHORT VERSION

I'm a technical founder with roots in computational research, now building climate tech infrastructure.

I spent two years developing CNN models for microbiome analysis and building CUDA-based molecular dynamics simulators. That research background taught me how to approach hard problems systematically.

Now I'm channeling that into entrepreneurship — specifically, building Project Zero to help companies navigate California's mandatory climate disclosure requirements (SB253/SB261).

WHY CLIMATE TECH

Through customer discovery, I found that most companies subject to these new regulations are woefully unprepared. They're tracking emissions in spreadsheets, hiring expensive consultants, and hoping for the best.

That's a real problem I can solve with software. Project Zero automates the entire compliance workflow — from data ingestion to Scope 1, 2, and 3 calculations to automated reporting.
