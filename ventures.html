Project Zero

Compliance infrastructure for California's climate disclosure mandates

THE PROBLEM

California's SB253 and SB261 require companies with $1B+ revenue to disclose their carbon emissions. Through customer discovery, I found that most companies are woefully unprepared — tracking emissions in spreadsheets, hiring expensive consultants, and lacking proper systems for Scope 3 supply chain data.

THE SOLUTION

Project Zero is a carbon accounting engine that:

  • Ingests company data from existing systems
  • Calculates Scope 1, 2, and 3 emissions automatically
  • Provides automated compliance reporting across multiple frameworks
  • Uses transaction-verified data instead of estimates

CURRENT STATUS

Active customer discovery phase. Cold outreach to sustainability and compliance leads at California companies with $1B+ revenue. Building MVP while validating product-market fit.

IN DEVELOPMENT

VertX

AI-native job search platform

THE PROBLEM

Job seekers get rejected by ATS systems before humans ever see their applications. I validated this pain point personally — 200+ applications with zero responses. The current job search process is opaque, inefficient, and frustrating.

THE SOLUTION

VertX is an AI-native job search platform that:

  • Provides personalized relevance scoring for job matches
  • Offers transparent feedback on application strength
  • Automates applications with intelligent optimization
  • Learns from outcomes to improve recommendations

CURRENT STATUS

Core platform architecture developed. Focusing on Zero Platform as primary venture while maintaining VertX for future development.
